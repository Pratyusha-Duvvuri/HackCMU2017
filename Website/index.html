Our Mission

This year alone millions of people have been impacted by hurricanes in the US. Hurricane Hero seeks to foster a community of people who help each other in time of need. We are a global community, we need to help and support each other. Please take the time to give needed resources for victims of the hurricanes or donate to charities like American Red Cross. Every person counts. If you are here to seek help, please go to out chatbot Hurricane Hurry to seek for the closet shelter, resources, and status updates in your area.
